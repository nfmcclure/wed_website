Accommodations

A hotel block has been reserved at the DoubleTree Missoula Edgewater. We did not reserve a block at the Holiday Inn Missoula Downtown, but you may also consider staying there. We selected these hotels because they provide easy walking access to the riverfront and downtown, which will make for a delightful stay. We recommend booking sooner rather than later, as Missoula is a popular destination and there are many other events throughout the summer. Shuttle service to the wedding venue will be provided from the DoubleTree hotel (the two hotels are very close to each other). The wedding venue is a 25-minute drive west of Missoula.
